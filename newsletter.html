March 13, 2020

Walker: John Jone

The dogs loved being outside on a Thursday with great weather. Bailey, our resident labrador retriever, got to walk past and witness the smells of the amazing Magnolia Bakery. Thursday, our favorite goldendoodle, chased after tennis balls in the park. Meanwhile, Jax watched the youngsters play as he enjoyed his retirement in the grass at Newsbridge Park.

March 24, 2020

Walker: Bailey Bowe

It was a little muddy today, but the dogs persevered. Mike, our sweet pitbull, seemed happy to be out of the house and took some time to roll around in the wet grass. Clifford, our ginger maltipoo, got a little too excited when we passed by the family of gray squirrels cuddled up in the great tree by the Inwood Library. These pups had a great day!
